About Me

I am a User Experience Designer based in New York with a passion for Product Design, Interaction Design, UI, Graphic Design. I care about bringing user-friendly design concepts to small and large businesses.

I started my academic career as a Computer Engineer and happened to take a course in User Experience Design during my grad school. I fell in love with the idea of UX, the value it adds to a product and how it plays such a pivotal role in determining the success of a product. Creativity has always been a huge part of who I am and using this with the technical aspect every single day has helped me deliver meaningful design solutions.

My Work

I have been working as a UI/UX Designer at ETR, an enterprise technology startup focused on delivering up-to-date Data Research to connect the IT end users. I work with the engineering team, the analytics team, stakeholders and the marketing team, to design delightful experiences for the CIOs and CTOs of Fortune 500 companies across mobile, web, and large-screen displays.

I tried to rate my skills based on how good I am at them, but I failed miserably. I really do believe that learning is a continuous process, and that if you are perfect at something, you haven't really given it enough time. I have learnt some tricks and skills over time though. To know more about it, please do check out my Resume.

What Sets me Apart

As an employee at a startup company, I was required to wear numerous hats and that drastically helped me hone my skills. As a designer with an understanding of code, the more I bring what I know from programming into my design practice, the faster I am able to move and iterate over the duration of the process. Working so close with the dev team at ETR familiarized me with multiple platforms, and my technical knowledge enabled ease of collaboration between the design team and the dev team. Through the engineer-designer feedback loop, considering the engineering constraints and designing for edge cases, I grow as a designer everyday. Working in a data research firm has also made me a strong proponent of data-driven design.
